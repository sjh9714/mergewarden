# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in MergeWarden welcoming and harassment-free,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity or expression, experience level, education, socioeconomic
status, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing observable impact.
- Accept feedback and correct mistakes.
- Keep security reports private until coordinated disclosure is appropriate.
- Focus criticism on code, policy, evidence, and behavior—not people.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, sexual
attention, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer through the contact
method on their GitHub profile. For security-sensitive material, use the
repository's private vulnerability reporting channel instead.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when behavior is inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.
Reports will be reviewed as promptly and confidentially as practical.
